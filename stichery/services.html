ALTERATION:

Here are some common types of dress alterations that can be performed:

  • Hemming: Adjusting the length of the dress by shortening or lengthening the hemline. This is a common alteration, especially for dresses that are too long or too short.
  • Taking in or letting out the sides:Modifying the width of the dress by taking it in (making it smaller) or letting it out (making it larger).
  • Adjusting the neckline:Altering the neckline of a dress can dramatically change its appearance.
  • Adding or removing sleeves:If the dress has sleeves, they can be shortened, lengthened, or completely removed based on your preference.
  • Adding or removing embellishments: Embellishments like sequins, beads, lace, or appliques can be added or removed to alter the overall design of the dress.
